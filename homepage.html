about us

Film Store offers the best quality film scanning services in Vietnam and we cherish each of your films as if they were our own.
We start doing this work with passion, and will pursue to the end for expanding the growing shooting film community

lamination process

We use Noritsu V50 film scanning machine which has the high quality and stability for C-41 process. For the other processes, we use our own automatic mini scanning machine made by us in order to scan compatibly with all types of film.

quality assurance

We use genuine chemicals and deionized pure water for all lamination processes. This gives the highest stability and quality to the film.

scan service

Fuji Frontier SP-3000 and Noritsu HS-1800, two leading scanners currently used by most major labs in the world to scan 35mm and 120.
In addition, the Lab also has Epson V850 Pro to scan LargeFormat films.
